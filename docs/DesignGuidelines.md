DESIGN ANTI-GOALS
- Do not make this feel like a habit-tracking hustle app
- Do not use shame, streak-loss anxiety, or guilt in reminders
- Do not overuse gradients, glassmorphism, or decorative motion
- Do not make the AI sound overly cheerful, clingy, or therapist-like

MICROCOPY RULES
- Writing should feel soft, intelligent, and emotionally grounded
- Avoid cliché wellness language
- Prefer short, reflective prompts over long inspirational speeches
- Error states should feel reassuring, never alarming

EMPTY STATES
- Every major screen should have an empty state with comforting, hopeful copy
- Empty states should invite reflection, not pressure action

REMINDER STYLE
- Notifications must be gentle invitations, not commands
- Examples:
  - “A quiet moment to appreciate someone?”
  - “Would you like to check in with yourself today?”
  - “A small step toward something meaningful is still a step.”

AI SAFETY BEHAVIOR
- The AI is supportive, reflective, and boundaried
- It must not imply dependency
- It must not present itself as a therapist or crisis responder
- Crisis situations should trigger a dedicated safety flow
